## Avoir - To Have
- Avoir is the verb that means to Have

## Avoir conjugation

- ***J'ai'*** – I am
- ***Tu as*** – You are
- ***Il a*** – He is
- ***Elle a*** – She is
- ***On a*** – It is
- ***Nous avons*** – We are
- ***Vous avez*** – You all are
- ***Ils ont*** – They are {Masculine}
- ***Elles ont*** – They are {Feminine}
