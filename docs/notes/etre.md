## Être - To Be
- Être is the verb that means to be

## Être conjugation

- ***Je Suis*** – I am
- ***Tu es*** – You are
- ***Il est*** – He is
- ***Elle est*** – She is
- ***On est*** – It is
- ***Nous sommes*** – We are
- ***Vous etes*** – You all are
- ***Ils sont*** – They are {Masculine}
- ***Elles sont*** – They are {Feminine}
