## Prepositions in French

- ***Sur*** - On
- ***Sous*** - Under
- ***à côté de*** -  Next to ***or*** Beside *(feminine)*
- ***Entre*** - Between
- ***à droite*** - To the right of 
- ***à gauche du*** - To The Left of
- ***à côté du*** - Next to ***or*** Beside *(Masculine)*
- ***devant*** - Infront
- ***en face de*** - infront of
- ***derrière*** - behind
